A typical UPSC CSE annual cycle

Exact dates change every year — always confirm against the official notification on upsc.gov.in — but the sequence below is broadly consistent.

StageTypical WindowFormat
NotificationFebruaryOnline application opens
Preliminary ExamMay – JuneObjective, 2 papers
Prelims Result~1–2 months laterQualifying roll numbers
Main ExaminationSeptemberWritten, 9 papers
Mains Result~3–4 months laterShortlist for Interview
Personality TestFollowing monthsInterview, 275 marks
Final ResultCycle closeMerit list & allocation
